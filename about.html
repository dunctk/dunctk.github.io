About Duncan Trevithick

Duncan Trevithick is a content producer and marketing automation specialist. Duncan has worked with some of the world’s leading brands including VMWare, QuickBooks, NetSuite, VEVO, John Lewis and many more. His marketing automation resume / CV is available here.

Duncan has also produced high-end events for international clients at Albert Hall in London. He filmed music videos for VEVO in London and around the UK which have generated millions of views online.

Duncan is passionate about sustainability having created an environment and renewable energy focused site and YouTube channel generating several thousand organic views and visitors each month via SEO optimized content.
